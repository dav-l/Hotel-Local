About HotelLocal

Welcome to HotelLocal! Our web app is designed to help travelers find locally owned and authentic hotels. We believe in promoting family owned business over the competition of high end franchise.

All of our hotels are locally owned and one of a kind, providing guests with the most authentic experience. Our hotels, with a range of ratings from one to five stars, provide all amenities without the tags of a franchise. Currently, we have cities limited to California. But our database is always expanding and we hope to cover all of America soon!
